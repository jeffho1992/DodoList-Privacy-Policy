DodoList Privacy Policy
Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction
DodoList ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal task management application developed by MegaTurbine.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, stored, and protected when you use the DodoList app.

By downloading and using DodoList, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect
DodoList stores information locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal information to our servers.

The app may store the following information locally:

Task information: titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, categories, completion status, and project assignments
Project information: project names, colors, icons, and associated tasks
App settings: display name, password, dark mode preference, notification preference, and default sort order
Usage statistics: task completion counts and productivity scores (calculated locally)
3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used only to provide the core functionality of the app:

To display, organize, and manage your tasks and projects
To calculate activity statistics and productivity scores
To apply your preferred app settings (theme, notifications, sorting)
To support backup and restore features you initiate through your device
4. Data Storage and Security
All data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database and SharedPreferences.

We do not operate any cloud servers
We do not store your data on external servers
Data is protected by your device's own security measures
Important: The app includes Android's backup functionality (allowBackup="true"). If you have device backup enabled, your data may be included in your Google account or device backup. This is managed by your device and Google, not by DodoList.

5. Third-Party Services
DodoList does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The app uses the following standard Android and open-source libraries:

Android Jetpack (Room, LiveData, ViewModel, Navigation, etc.)
Material Design Components
MPAndroidChart (for local statistics charts)
Timber (for debug logging in development builds only)
These libraries process data locally on your device and do not transmit data to external services.

6. Permissions
The app may request the following permissions:

Notifications: optional, used for task reminders (if implemented in future updates)
The app does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or external storage.

7. Children's Privacy
DodoList is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights
You have full control over your data:

You can add, edit, complete, or delete tasks at any time
You can clear completed tasks through the Settings screen
You can log out / clear all local app data through the Settings screen
You can uninstall the app to remove all data from your device
9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
